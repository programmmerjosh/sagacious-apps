Q: What is the purpose of the Goal Symmetry app?

A: The purpose of this app is to solve a few common problems most people face. Problems such as: 1. Not having a reliable & efficient goal setting system. 2. Not having a safe method of storage of your goals - most goal setters either lose their goals and notes due to clutter or misplacing their journals and notebooks over time. 3. Not having reminders when goal deadlines are approaching. 4. Not having a neat documentation tool that links your goals, milestones, notes and dates all together appropriately and beautifully.

Q: How does Goal Symmetry work?

A: Goal Symmetry was designed in such a way that everything is already set up for you. There are divisions: Short Term, Long Term And Dreams. Within each of those divisions, are categories: Financial, Health, Skills, Family etc. Within each of those categories, you can create your very own goal lists accordingly. Once a goal is created, you can setup a deadline for each goal, update each goal's progress at any time, create milestones and/or notes and more.

Q: What happens to my goals if I delete Goal Symmetry or lose my device?

A: Rest assured your goals will be safe and sound on a secure Firebase database. When you login with your username and password, on any Android or iOS device that can run the Goal Symmetry app, you'll have full access-to and control-of your goals, goal notes & goal milestones.
